The Conference

JHipster Code isn't your usual conference: we come here to code, learn, share and eventually contribute to Open Source.

We believe that you learn better by doing, that coding is a social (and fun!) activity, and we want to help improve Open Source projects that we love.

Most of the conference will be spent in small groups, under the supervision of Core Team Members of the JHipster project, where you will be able to code with your peers.

  • The conference will take place in Bordeaux, France, on Friday, June 26th, 2020
  • The conference is organized by the JHipster Developers Association, a French non-profit entity ("association loi 1901")

Sponsorship information

JHipster Code is organized by the JHipster Developers Association, a French non-profit organization. As such, we are all volunteers and do not make money out of the conference.

But organizing the conference costs money, which is clearly not covered by the ticket prices.

Here are the sponsorship details:

Sponsorship price is 2,500 Euros (VAT included - remember we are a non-profit? We don't pay VAT!).
Sponsors will get a booth at the venue.
Sponsors will have a 3-minute slot during the opening keynote.
Sponsors will have 5 free seats.
